To start I choose the Ethical Hacking: Understanding Ethical Hacking course. It's very interesting. Below are some facts that I learned on the course:

  • By 2017 the Global Cyber Security Market is expected to sky-rocket to USD120.1 Billion from USD 64.7 Billion in 2011.
  • Estimated Annual cost of Global Cybercrime is USD 375 Billion - If you took all the revenues from the top technology companies (Microsoft, Google, Amazon, Ebay, Yahoo, Apple and Netflix) combined you wouldn't get close to what Cybercrime is doing.
  • Cybercrime Victims per year - 556 million. Breaking down it's 1.5 million per day or 18 victims per second.
  • Over 657 Million of Identities Exposed (40 Million victims from USA, 54 million victims from Turkey).
  • 2008 - 1 billion devices connected to the internet.
  • 2014 - 1.4 trillion devices connected to the internet.
  • 2015 - 30% increase = 4.9 Trillion devices on the internet.
  • More than 600.000 Facebook accounts are compromised every single day.
  • 59% ex-employees admit to steal company data when they leave their jobs.
